Research Focus

My PhD at the Georg Nemetschek Institute at TUM explores how artificial intelligence can transform the built environment through probabilistic digital twins.

The central question: How do we combine all available data in an engineering process to improve decision making? This involves bridging the gap between physical infrastructure and digital representations, enabling smarter, data-driven decisions across the entire lifecycle of buildings and infrastructure.
